META: FRONT-END DEVELOPMENT PROFESSIONAL CERTIFICATE
This Certificate is awarded to learners who complete a series of courses on front-end web development, hosted by Coursera. With a focus on React.js, these rigorous, self-paced courses, developed by Meta experts, prepare a candidate for an entry level job as a front-end developer. The courses culminate in the successful completion of a capstone project.
